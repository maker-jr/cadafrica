Our brief biography.

The Centre for Advocacy & Development in Africa (CADA) formally known as Campaign Against Drug Abuse is a Youth focused NGO established in 2000 by some Members of National Drug Law Enforcement Agency (NDLEA) Drug Free Club from various Higher Institutions across the Country after the Official Lunch of the Club at the Institute of Management & Technology IMT Enugu, by the NDLEA Chairman / CE.


In 2005, the Organization reviewed its mandate and objective(s) to accommodate other issues of National and International importance, especially the Millennium Development Goals MDG’s target. Presently, Our Mandate is to support and encourage Youth and Students in Educational Research Projects, foster Peace initiatives amongst the African Youth, and to provide a platform where the African Youth can exchange ideas, and as well assist developmental programs aimed at poverty reduction in Africa.


The Organization is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission CAC / IT / 24948 and various State Youth Department in Nigeria and within the Africa Continent, as well as other relevant Agencies. It is certified by the National Drug Law Enforcement Agency (NDLEA) Nigeria as a complimentary effort to its activities on Drug Demand Reduction amongst the Youth.

.

As a Youth Research and Development Centre we have Organized Programs and carried out Projects in line with the African Union AU - New Economic Partnership for African Development (NEPAD) targeted at Young People of Africa, in relation to the Millennium Development Goal’s and now the Sustainable Development Goals SDGs in Africa.


Realizes that Young People are necessary agent for change and Sustainable Development, CADA has initiated and organized several seminars, workshops and lecture series for students and young people in the Private, State and Federal Schools, as well as Higher Institutions in different Countries of Africa.

It is as a result of our commitment and resourcefulness on Youth Matters across the Africa Continent that the UNHCR has been our major partners in organizing the annual African Youth Peace Conference in commemoration of the UN World Peace Day across Africa, which was initiated by CADA in 2006 and supported by the World Bank - Nigeria.
