ACHIEVEMENTS

WHAT WE HAVE ACHIEVED SO FAR


Realizing that Young People are necessary agent for change and Sustainable Development, CADA has initiated and organized several seminars, workshops and lecture series for Students and Young People in the Private, State and Federal Schools, as well as Higher Institutions in different Countries of Africa.

It is as a result of our commitment and resourcefulness on Youth and Student Matters across the Africa Continent that the United Nations System in Africa and other major partners have been supporting CADA to (Annually & Biannually) organize the following events across the Africa Continent:

  • African Youth Peace Conference in commemoration of the United Nations World Peace Day Annually across Africa, supported by The World Bank – Africa, Federal Ministry of Youth & Sports, Institute for Peace and Conflict Resolution – Presidency and UNHCR.
  • African Students Leaders Conference, Biannually supported by UNICEF, NDLEA, Ministries of Foreign Affairs and Society for Family Health International
