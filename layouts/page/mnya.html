August 12, 2019
CADA

August 12, every Year since 1990 is set aside for the observation of the United Nations International Youth Day, with wide range of activities to raise awareness about the situation of Youth in Member Countries, in which all Youth and Youth Organizations around the World, Stakeholders, Government at all levels, the Organized Private Sectors, the International Community, and Non Governmental Organizations are involved.


It is in pursuance of the objective of this day, that the Centre for Advocacy & Development in Africa CADA and the United Nations African Youth Peace Ambassadors – Nigeria have initiated the Miss Nigeria Youth Ambassador (MNYA) Gala and Award Ceremony, partnering with other Youth Stakeholders to commemorate the 2019 International Youth Day and to promote the effort of Young Women with Brain and Beauty as one of the most productive resources in achieving the Sustainable Development Goals as well as the United Nations Youth Agenda in Nigeria, using a Social Event.

THE DUTIES OF MISS NIGERIA YOUTH AMBASSADOR 2019

The Queen will be expected to:


  • Offer her time on a voluntary basis to address Youth Issues across the Country and participate in major Youth Programmes and Activities during her tenure.
  • Familiarize herself with the activities of Youth Organizations, NYCN, Pan African Youth Union Programmes, World Assembly of Youth Programmes, United Nations Youth Programme, and the relevant Ministries by being able to articulate basic Cultural Youth Issues at the core of Sustainable Development Goals.
  • Attend Private Sector, Government and UN Youth Workshops, Seminars and Conference if requested
  • Represent Nigeria Youth at the Regional, Continental and Global Contest of Miss Africa Youth Pageant
  • Assist meeting the Project’s stated goals and objectives by participating in educational, policy, or performance events
  • Adhere to communication guidelines, which include awareness of the UN International Youth Day, as well as timely responses to communiqués
  • Appear at major events such as UN World Programmes on Youth, African Youth Celebration and World Urban Youth Assembly and World Assembly of Youth WAY
  • Maintain a web page, web log, or similar on the social activities she is engaged in, that is linked to the Nigerian Youth Goodwill Ambassador and other partners website
